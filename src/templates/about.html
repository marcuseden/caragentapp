About CarAgentApp

Our Mission

CarAgentApp helps car buyers find the best deals across different markets by comparing prices and identifying arbitrage opportunities. Our platform aggregates car listings from multiple countries, normalizes the data, and presents it in a way that makes cross-border comparisons easy.

Whether you're a private buyer looking for the best deal or a dealer seeking arbitrage opportunities, CarAgentApp provides the tools and insights you need to make informed decisions.

How It Works

1

Data Collection

We scrape car listings from multiple sources across different countries.

2

Data Normalization

We standardize the data to make cross-border comparisons possible.

3

Price Analysis

We identify price differences for similar cars across different markets.

4

Opportunity Identification

We highlight significant price differences that represent potential arbitrage opportunities.
